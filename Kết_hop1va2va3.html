QKTD + QKĐ: QKTD là nền (while/when) + QKĐ gián đoạn.

🔮 Nâng Cao: Mệnh Đề Trạng Ngữ Thời Gian & Stative Verbs

Sau liên từ: when, while, as soon as, until, before, after, by the time — KHÔNG dùng thì tương lai, thay bằng HTĐ hoặc HTHT.

❌ SAI
When she will come, I will cook.
✅ ĐÚNG
When she comes, I will cook.

Stative Verbs — KHÔNG dùng V-ing:

  • Cảm xúc: love, hate, like, prefer, want, wish
  • Nhận thức: know, believe, understand, remember, forget
  • Giác quan: see, hear, smell, taste, feel
  • Sở hữu: have, own, belong, possess

💡 Nguyên tắc phân biệt cốt lõi
  • Có mốc thời gian cụ thể (yesterday, last week, in 2020, at 5pm…) → bắt buộc dùng QKĐ
  • Dùng since + mốc thời gian cụ thể → HTHT: I have lived here since 2018.
  • Dùng for + khoảng thời gian → HTHT (còn tiếp diễn): She has studied here for 3 years.
  • Câu hỏi "When did…?" luôn dùng QKĐ — không bao giờ dùng HTHT sau "when" hỏi mốc cụ thể.
Dạng 1
QKĐ có "ago" → HTHT với "for / since"

📌 QKĐ → HTHT (dùng for)

QKĐ: S + V2 + thời gian + ago → HTHT: S + have/has + (been + V-ing / V3) + for + khoảng thời gian
  • She started studying English 5 years ago.
    → She has studied English for 5 years.
  • He began working here 3 months ago.
    → He has been working here for 3 months.
  • I bought this phone 2 years ago.
    → I have had this phone for 2 years.

📌 QKĐ → HTHT (dùng since)

QKĐ: S + started/began + V-ing/to V + in + năm/tháng → HTHT: S + have/has + V3 + since + năm/tháng
  • They began to live here in 2010.
    → They have lived here since 2010.
  • She started working there in January.
    → She has worked there since January.
  • I moved to HCMC in 2019.
    → I have lived in HCMC since 2019.
📋 Tóm tắt Dạng 1
Cấu trúc QKĐCấu trúc HTHT tương đươngNghĩa

⚠️ FOR vs SINCE
  • FOR + khoảng thời gian (duration): for 3 years, two weeks, a long time
  • SINCE + mốc thời gian cụ thể: since 2020, last year, Monday, I was a child
Dạng 2
HTHT dùng "the last time / It has been / last" → HTHT phủ định
QKĐ có thể ở các dạng: It has been + thời gian + since + S + last + V2 S + last + V2 + time + ago The last time + S + V2 + was + time + ago → HTHT: S + haven't/hasn't + V3 + for/since + thời gian

Dùng "started/began" → HTHT (lần cuối)

  • I last met him 2 months ago.
    → I haven't met him for 2 months.
  • She last visited us in 2021.
    → She hasn't visited us since 2021.
  • He last called me a week ago.
    → He hasn't called me for a week.

Dùng "The last time / It has been"

  • The last time I saw her was 3 years ago.
    → I haven't seen her for 3 years.
  • It has been 5 years since I last visited Paris.
    → I haven't visited Paris for 5 years.
  • It is 3 years since I last saw her.
    → I haven't seen her for 3 years.
🔮 4 Công thức chuyển đổi với "started/began/last"

Công thức 1: started/began + ago

QKĐ: S + started/began + V-ing/to V + time + ago → HTHT: S + have/has + V3 + for + time
She started studying English 5 years ago.
→ She has studied English for 5 years.

Công thức 2: started/began + in năm

QKĐ: S + started/began + V-ing/to V + in năm/tháng → HTHT: S + have/has + V3 + since + năm/tháng
They began to live here in 2010.
→ They have lived here since 2010.

Công thức 3: last + V2 + time + ago

QKĐ: S + last + V2 + time + ago → HTHT: S + haven't/hasn't + V3 + for + time
I last met him 2 months ago.
→ I haven't met him for 2 months.

Công thức 4: It is/has been + since

QKĐ: It was + time + since + S + last + V2 → HTHT: It has been + time + since + S + last + V2 (Hoặc: S + have/has + not + V3 + for + time)
It is 3 years since I last saw her.
→ I haven't seen her for 3 years.

Dạng 3
Câu hỏi "When did…?" / "When was the last time…?" → "How long…?"

QKĐ (câu hỏi)

When did + S + V-infinitive? When was the last time + S + V2?

→ HTHT (câu hỏi)

How long + have/has + S + (been + V-ing / V3)? (Động từ trạng thái: How long + have/has + S + had + N?)

Ví dụ chuyển đổi

🔮 Lưu ý quan trọng về V-ing vs V3
Dùng have/has been + V-ing

Khi động từ chỉ hành động kéo dài liên tục: live, work, study, wait, rain, teach, learn…

  • How long have you been living here?
  • How long has she been studying?
  • How long have they been waiting?
Dùng have/has + V3 (không -ing)

Khi động từ là stative verb (trạng thái, sở hữu): have, know, own, like, be…

  • How long have you known her?
  • How long has he had this car?
  • How long has she been a teacher?

🔮 Luyện Tập Tổng Hợp — 6 Mẫu Hay Gặp Trong Bài Thi

Mẫu 1: ago → for

  • I moved here 6 months ago.
    → I have lived here for 6 months.

Mẫu 2: ago → since (có mốc)

  • She started working there in 2020.
    → She has worked there since 2020.

Mẫu 3: last → haven't for

  • I last saw Tom a year ago.
    → I haven't seen Tom for a year.

Mẫu 4: The last time → haven't since

  • The last time I ate pizza was 2 weeks ago.
    → I haven't eaten pizza for 2 weeks.

Mẫu 5: It is since → haven't for

  • It is 5 years since I last visited Paris.
    → I haven't visited Paris for 5 years.

Mẫu 6: When did → How long have

  • When did you start learning guitar?
    → How long have you been learning guitar?
🚫 Những Động Từ Không Chia Tiếp Diễn (Stative Verbs)
⚠️
Stative Verbs — Không dùng V-ing, luôn dùng thì đơn

Đây là các động từ trạng thái — diễn tả trạng thái/cảm xúc/nhận thức, không phải hành động đang diễn ra. Dù ngữ cảnh nào cũng KHÔNG dùng -ing với các động từ này.

❤️ Cảm xúc / Ý chí
  • love, hate, like, dislike
  • prefer, want, wish, need
  • hope, care, mind, fear
  • adore, desire, detest
🧠 Nhận thức / Tư duy
  • know, believe, think (*)
  • understand, realize
  • remember, forget, doubt
  • suppose, assume, imagine
👁️ Giác quan thụ động
  • see (*), hear (*)
  • smell (*), taste (*)
  • feel (*), notice, seem
  • appear, look (*), sound
📦 Sở hữu / Quan hệ
  • have (*), own, possess
  • belong, contain, include
  • consist of, involve
  • depend on, concern
⚠️ (*) Một số động từ có 2 nghĩa — nghĩa khác nhau, cách dùng khác nhau
Động từStative (không dùng -ing)Dynamic (dùng được -ing)
thinkI think you are right. (tôi cho rằng)I am thinking about the problem. (đang suy nghĩ)
haveShe has a nice house. (sở hữu)She is having lunch. (đang ăn trưa)
seeI see what you mean. (hiểu)I am seeing a doctor tomorrow. (gặp)
lookShe looks tired. (trông có vẻ)She is looking at the map. (đang nhìn)
smellThe soup smells delicious. (có mùi)The dog is smelling the flowers. (đang ngửi)
tasteThis tastes amazing. (có vị)The chef is tasting the soup. (đang nếm)
🕰️ Used to — Be used to — Get used to
used to + V-inf
Cơ bản
(+) S + used to + V-inf (-) S + didn't use to + V-inf (?) Did + S + use to + V-inf?

Ý nghĩa: Thói quen hoặc trạng thái trong QUÁ KHỨ — bây giờ không còn nữa.

I used to live in Hanoi. (trước sống ở HN, giờ không)
She used to smoke but she quit. (trước hút thuốc)
He didn't use to like vegetables, but now he does.
Did you use to play football as a kid?
💡 Lưu ý

Chỉ dùng ở QUÁ KHỨ. Không có dạng hiện tại "use to" với ý nghĩa này.

be used to + V-ing/N
Cơ bản
(+) S + am/is/are/was/were + used to + V-ing/N (-) S + am/is/are + not used to + V-ing/N

Ý nghĩa: Đã quen với điều gì đó (trạng thái quen rồi — có thể ở HT hoặc QK).

I am used to waking up early. (tôi quen dậy sớm rồi)
She is used to the cold weather. (cô ấy quen lạnh rồi)
He wasn't used to driving on the left. (hắn chưa quen lái trái)
get used to + V-ing/N
Nâng cao
(+) S + get/gets/got + used to + V-ing/N

Ý nghĩa: Đang trở nên quen / dần quen với điều gì đó (quá trình).

I am getting used to living alone. (đang dần quen)
She got used to the new job quickly. (đã dần quen)
It takes time to get used to a new culture.

⏳ Liên Từ Thời Gian: After · Before · Until · When · As soon as · While
AFTER
after + gì?

Cấu trúc

  • After + S + V (QKĐ/HTHT): After she finished / had finished, she left.
  • After + V-ing: After finishing dinner, he watched TV.
  • After + N: After the meeting, we had lunch.
  • After + that: We had lunch. After that, we went for a walk.
⚠️ Quy tắc thì

Sau "after" dùng HTĐ/HTHT (không dùng TL): After she arrives, we will eat. ✅

Ví dụ đầy đủ

  • After I had eaten dinner, I went for a walk.
  • After studying for 3 hours, she took a break.
  • After the exam, everyone felt relieved.
  • We'll discuss the results after the presentation ends.
BEFORE
before + gì?

Cấu trúc

  • Before + S + V (HTĐ/QKĐ): Before she leaves / left...
  • Before + V-ing: Before going to bed, brush your teeth.
  • Before + N: Before the test, review your notes.
⚠️ Quy tắc thì

Sau "before" KHÔNG dùng TL: Before she comes, clean the room. ✅ (không phải "will come")

Ví dụ đầy đủ

  • Before I go to sleep, I always read a book.
  • He had never cooked before he lived alone.
  • Before leaving the house, she locked the door.
  • Please finish your homework before dinner.
UNTIL
until / till + gì?

Cấu trúc

  • Until + S + V (HTĐ/QKĐ): Wait until she calls.
  • Until + N: She waited until midnight.
  • Not...until: He didn't stop until he finished.
💡 Ý nghĩa

"Until" = "cho đến khi". Diễn đạt hành động tiếp tục CHO ĐẾN MỘT ĐIỂM DỪNG cụ thể.

Ví dụ đầy đủ

  • I'll wait here until you come back. (sẽ chờ đến khi bạn về)
  • She didn't go home until the party ended.
  • Don't leave until you finish your meal.
  • He kept running until he couldn't anymore.
WHEN
when · as soon as

Cấu trúc & phân biệt

WHEN — "khi"
Hành động chính xảy ra tại/sau thời điểm mô tả.
When I arrived, she was cooking.
AS SOON AS — "ngay khi"
Hành động chính xảy ra ngay lập tức sau.
As soon as I arrive, I'll call you.
⚠️ QUAN TRỌNG — Không dùng TL sau when/as soon as

When she comes home, I will cook. ✅ | When she will come home... ❌

Ví dụ đầy đủ

  • When it rains, I stay inside. (thói quen HTĐ)
  • When I was young, I used to play outside. (QK)
  • As soon as she gets home, she'll call. (TG → HTĐ)
  • He smiled as soon as he saw her. (QK)
WHILE
while — "trong khi"

Cấu trúc

  • While + S + was/were + V-ing → hành động đang xảy ra trong quá khứ (nền)
  • While + S + V-ing (rút gọn) → chủ ngữ 2 câu trùng nhau
  • While + S + HTĐ → diễn tả 2 việc song song hiện tại
💡 while vs when

while = trong suốt khoảng thời gian đang diễn ra (V-ing). when = tại một thời điểm (thường QKĐ làm gián đoạn).

Ví dụ đầy đủ

  • While she was cooking, I set the table. (2 việc song song QK)
  • I dropped my phone while I was walking. (gián đoạn)
  • While watching TV, he fell asleep. (rút gọn cùng chủ ngữ)
  • Some people like tea while others prefer coffee. (tương phản)
BY THE TIME
by the time + gì?

Cấu trúc

  • By the time + HTĐ → sẽ + HTHT TL: By the time she arrives, I will have left.
  • By the time + QKĐ → QKHT: By the time he came, she had gone.

Ví dụ đầy đủ

  • By the time you read this, I will have left.
  • By the time we arrived, the film had already started.
  • By the time she finishes school, she'll have studied for 16 years.

Câu Bị Động Passive Voice

📌 Công thức chung

Chủ động: S + V + O → Bị động: S(mới) + Be(chia thì) + V3/ed + (by + agent)

Chủ động
Tom writes a letter.

A letter is written by Tom.

📋 4 Bước chuyển đổi
  • Bước 1: Tân ngữ (O) của câu chủ động → làm chủ ngữ câu bị động.

🔮 Đảo Ngữ, Unless, Provided, As long as

🔮 Các mẫu chuyển đổi hay gặp trong đề thi

It is + adj + to-V → V-ing + is + adj

  • It is important to study hard.
    → Studying hard is important.
  • It is interesting to learn English.
    → Learning English is interesting.

Too + adj + to-V → so + adj + that + can't

  • He is too tired to work.
    → He is so tired that he can't work.
  • The box is too heavy to lift.
    → The box is so heavy that I can't lift it.

adj + enough + to-V → so + adj + that + can

  • She is old enough to drive.
    → She is so old that she can drive.
  • He is strong enough to carry it.
    → He is so strong that he can carry it.

In order to / So as to → In order that / So that

  • She studied hard in order to pass.
    → She studied hard so that she could pass.
  • He spoke slowly so as to be understood.
    → He spoke slowly so that everyone could understand.
